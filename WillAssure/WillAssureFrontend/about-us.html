About Us

Welcome to will assure, we here to cater to your needs online. Making a will is always top of the mind for each one of us, but we are hesitant/ reluctant to do so as all the other priorities are humming, and we do not find time to go to an advocate.
Will assure team understands your difficulties and has decided to assist you with this online facility. Make your will from where you wish, no restrictions of geographical boundaries. This need was reckoned by our team, Adv. Dilip Rajput and Dr. Medha Shetye . Our team comprises of qualified professionals which facilitates in making a valid will. Our team is committed to our customers and thrive to reach to their expectations.
The blend of legal knowledge and the expertise of our developers facilitates our users to easily draft their own will answering the basic questions and deliver quality services to each of our users.

Meet Our Team

We are here to Acelerate your business and help you find the way.

Adv. Dilip Rajput

Founder of Will Assure and the Adian group, Dilip has spent over two and half decades of playing a host of roles in the capital market and investment management domains. His multi-faceted interests can be judged by his Masters Degrees in Management and Law coupled with Certified Financial Planner (CFP CM).
He has to credit setting up and managing large operations teams in wealth management and capital market fields. He has significant experience in large scale projects related to operational and financial process reengineering, software scale ups and technology upgrades.
After stints with the Tata Group and thereafter with Kotak Group Dilip founded Adian in 2014.

Dr. Adv Medha Shetye

Co Founder and CEO of Will Assure , Qualified Legal professional with a doctorate in law and a master’s degree in marketing management.
A practicing lawyer with a rich experience of over two decades in the legal field primarily focussed on matters related succession planning, Wills, Probate of wills, succession certificates, Letter of Administration and Private Trusts.
Qualified and chartered trusts and estate planner, from American Academy of Financial Management. Dynamic and motivated professional with a proven record of generating and building relationships, managing team, designing educational strategies, and coaching individuals to success. A committed senior lecturer with over 10 years of teaching experience in reputed institutions. Former principal of a law college.
