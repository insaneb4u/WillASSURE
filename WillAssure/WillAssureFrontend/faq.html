Who can be the executor?

Executor can be any individual above the age of 18 years, in whom the testator reposes faith for the execution of the will

Who can be the guardian?

Guardian can be any individual above the age of 18 years, appointed by the testator for the care and custody and wellbeing of his minor children

What do you mean by attestation?

Attestation is a declaration that the document is executed in the presence of witness. The witness attests the will.

Is registration mandatory?

Registration for the will is not mandatory.

Can alterations be carried in the will?

Yes, you can alter your will any number of times in your life time.

Can it be through a Codocil or new will?

Minor alterations can be done through a codicil, but for major changes it is advisable that you draft a new will

Which will shall be effective if multiple wills are made?

The last will of the testator shall take effect in case multiple wills are made by the testator

Difference between a living will and a will?

Will is the disposition of property of the testator after his death according to his desires. Living will is the desire of the maker of a living will as to what decisions are to be taken in case his health detoriates to a extent where he cannot take his own decisions about himself.

Who can be a beneficiary to the will?

Any family member, stranger or an institution can be a beneficiary to a will, it depends on the desire of the testator.

Is it advisable to make multiple copies of will?

It is not advisable to make multiple copies of the will

Will my personal information be secured?

Yes, data is encrypted and hence your personal information is secured.

Will my personal information stored?

Will assure gives an option to its client to select whether they would like to store the information or not. The storing of data if any would be strictly on your instructions only.

Where should I store my will?

Will can be stored with the registrar in a fire proof place

The online will generated by willassure, will it be called a valid document i.e recognized by law?

Yes, if you are filing the details accurately, the PDF generated is a valid document recognized by law.

Is the process of probate always essential for execution of a will?

The process of probate is not essential for an execution of will in all places in India

What will happen if I do not appoint executors?

If you do not appoint an executor the court will have to appoint administrator for the same.

If the executor is not appointed will the “will generated” be considered invalid?

No the will, shall not be considered invalid but it shall unnecessary lead to legal complications

Can I make a will for a portion of my property?

You may do so but is not advisable as the whole purpose of distribution of wealth as per your desires and to avoid legal complications becomes infructuous if you are doing it for a portion of the property.
